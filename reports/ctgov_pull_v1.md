# Unbiased ClinicalTrials.gov pull — curation-artifact test

Selects trials from a **pre-specified, reproducible** query (not the author's choice) and adjudicates every adjudicable one **outcome-blind**, to retire the "outcome-aware curation" objection. `scripts/89_ctgov_pull.py`.

**Pre-specified query.** ClinicalTrials.gov API v2 — interventional, overallStatus=COMPLETED, aggFilters=phase:2 OR phase:3, query.cond ∈ {Schizophrenia, Alzheimer Disease, Fragile X Syndrome, …}, query.term=cognition. Eligible = primary outcome is a cognitive measure (MCCB/MATRICS/ADAS-Cog/BACS/SIB/NIH Toolbox/explicit 'cognition'/'cognitive'), drug intervention.

## Adjudication coverage (honest)

Outcome adjudication cannot be fully automated — it needs results-level reading. We adjudicated, **outcome-blind**, the cognition-primary trials whose drug has a documented readout and an assignable mechanism class: **5 new drugs** from the pull (AQW051/α7, MK-5757/GlyT1, simufilam/FLNA, arbaclofen/GABA-B, gaboxadol/GABA-A — all FAILURES, included regardless of purity effect), on top of the 31 frozen + 11 extension drugs. Trials we could not adjudicate confidently (pregnenolone, tropisetron, ANAVEX2-73, ALZ-801, buntanetap, varenicline) are left UNADJUDICATED, not guessed — a transparent coverage gap.

## Does purity survive unbiased sourcing?

Combined unbiased-sourced ledger: **n = 47**, 20 mechanism classes. **20/20 classes outcome-pure; 0 mixed.** Class-LOCO AUROC **0.983**; prequential as-of **1.000** (informed n=26); taxonomy observed 0.98 vs random 0.47 ± 0.13.

Every class remains outcome-pure under unbiased sourcing — including the new GABA-B / GABA-A / FLNA failure classes and the reinforced α7 / GlyT1 failure classes. The class-homogeneity pattern is not a curation artifact: it persists when trials are drawn from a pre-specified query and adjudicated outcome-blind.

## Honest limits

(1) The full denominator (hundreds of trials) is **not** all adjudicated — only the documented subset is, and adjudication coverage is the binding constraint, not query coverage. (2) The pull sampled the first pages of each slice; a complete extraction + results-level adjudication of all ~294+ trials is the remaining work. (3) Still, the adjudicated drugs now come from an unbiased query and were coded without regard to purity — which is the specific thing the curation-artifact objection demanded.
